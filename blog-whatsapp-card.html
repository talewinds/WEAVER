How to Make a WhatsApp Greeting Card in 30 Seconds (Free & No App Needed)

Published on: 2025 • Written by Wish Weaver Team

Creating beautiful, personalised WhatsApp greeting cards doesn’t require installing big apps or paying for subscriptions. With Wish Weaver, you can create high-quality digital cards in just 30 seconds, directly from your browser.

Follow this simple step-by-step guide to make your first card instantly.

1. Open Wish Weaver

Visit the official Wish Weaver website:

https://talewinds.github.io/WEAVER/

No login required. No installation. Works on any smartphone or computer.

2. Upload Your Photo

Tap Upload Image and choose any picture:

  • Festival images
  • Family photos
  • Nature backgrounds
  • Saved greeting templates

All processing stays on your device — nothing gets uploaded.

3. Add Your Wish Text

Type your message in the “Main Wish” box. Some examples:

  • “Happy Birthday!”
  • “Happy Diwali!”
  • “Congratulations!”
  • “Good Morning!”

You can change the font, color, size, rotation, and position. Simply drag the text where you want it.

4. Add Stickers

Choose from fun emoji-style stickers. Just tap to add — drag to move — rotate to style.

Stickers export in high quality even on 4K screens.

5. Click “GENERATE SHARABLE CARD”

Wish Weaver instantly creates a high-resolution PNG. You can download or share it directly to:

  • WhatsApp Status
  • Direct Messages
  • Instagram
  • Facebook
  • Any sharing app

The image remains clean, crisp, and watermark-free.

Bonus Tips

  • 1:1 ratio is perfect for WhatsApp Status
  • 9:16 is ideal for Instagram Stories
  • 4:5 works well for portrait greeting designs
  • Use a combination of two fonts for stylish effects

Why Wish Weaver Is Better Than Apps

  • No app installation
  • No accounts or logins
  • No heavy ads
  • No watermarks
  • Works on any smartphone
