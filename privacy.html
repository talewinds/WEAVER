Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 2025

Welcome to Wish Weaver (https://talewinds.github.io/WEAVER/). Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use Wish Weaver, our browser-based greeting card creator.

1. Information We Collect

1.1. Images & Text You Upload

Wish Weaver does not upload your photos or text to any server. All editing happens directly in your browser (client-side). Your images stay on your device unless you choose to download or share them.

1.2. Local Storage

Wish Weaver may save your designs in your browser’s localStorage—for example:

  • Saved card designs
  • Preferred settings (fonts, colors, etc.)

This information never leaves your device.

2. Google AdSense & Cookies

Wish Weaver displays advertisements using Google AdSense. Google may use cookies or device identifiers to:

  • Serve personalised ads (where allowed by law)
  • Measure ad performance
  • Prevent fraud

You can control ad personalisation here:
Google Ads Settings

Users in the EU may see Google’s automatic cookie consent message.

3. Third-Party Services

We use:

  • Google AdSense – to show ads
  • GitHub Pages – to host Wish Weaver

These services may collect anonymous analytics such as:

  • Device type
  • Browser type
  • Country-level location
  • Usage patterns

We do not collect or store any personal data ourselves.

4. Children’s Privacy

Wish Weaver is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

5. External Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for their content or privacy practices.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version will always be available on this page.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions, you may contact us at:
